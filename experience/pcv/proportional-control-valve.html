Proportional Control Valve

Mechanical Engineering Project · December 2024

What is a Proportional Control Valve?

A proportional control valve is a type of hydraulic valve that controls fluid flow rate proportionally to an electrical input signal — unlike a simple on/off valve, it can continuously adjust flow, pressure, or direction based on a varying current or voltage input. By precisely regulating the spool position via an electromagnetic coil (solenoid), it achieves smooth, fine-grained control of hydraulic actuators (cylinders or motors), enabling variables such as cylinder velocity, output force, and motion trajectory to be modulated with high accuracy.

This project covers the complete product lifecycle: concept design, detailed CAD modeling, kinematic analysis, tolerance stack-up management, prototype testing, supply chain coordination, and production documentation — applying mechanical engineering fundamentals to a real-world industrial component.

电气比例控制阀

机械工程实训项目 · 2024年12月

什么是电气比例控制阀？

电气比例控制阀是一种根据电信号（电流或电压）连续调节液压油流量、压力或方向的阀门——与简单的开关阀不同，它能实现平滑、精细的液压执行器控制。通过电磁线圈通电产生的电磁力驱动阀芯移动，改变阀口开度，使输入信号与阀芯位移呈线性比例关系，从而精准控制油缸的速度、输出力和运动轨迹。

本项目涵盖电气比例控制阀的完整产品生命周期：概念设计、详细 CAD 建模、运动学分析、公差累积计算、原型测试、供应链协调与生产工艺文件编制，将机械工程基础知识应用于真实的工业零部件开发。
